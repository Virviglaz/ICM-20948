Detailed Description

RawData struct represents the raw sensor data read from the ICM-20948. It includes raw accelerometer, gyroscope, and temperature readings. The GetTemperature(), GetAccX(), GetAccY(), GetAccZ(), GetGyroX(), GetGyroY(), and GetGyroZ() methods convert the raw readings to real-world units (°C for temperature, g for acceleration, and °/s for angular velocity) based on the current gain settings.
